About

Project Robot Playground is a collection of simulation assets to make it easy to test your robot in realistic scenes. Each set includes highly curated models that have been optimized for simulation. All sets feature low-poly meshes with realistic textures, simple shape collisions, and realistic inertias and masses.

Best of all: It's all FREE

... even for commercial use.

  • Curated collection of simulation assets.

  • Commercial friendly license.

  • Gazebo integration and support.
